# Foundation Repair & Waterproofing – Sample Output

## Executive Summary

- US foundation repair & waterproofing market reached **$11.4B in 2024**, compounding at **5.8% CAGR** on the back of aging housing stock, heavier rainfall, and insurance-driven maintenance requirements. [[DOC-1-3]]
- Structural repair providers enjoy **28‑35% gross margins** while waterproofing specialists capture higher labor productivity (1.4 jobs/crew/day) thanks to repeatable installation kits. [[DOC-1-7]]
- Demand is concentrated in **Midwest & Mid‑Atlantic (43% of spend)** where freeze–thaw cycles accelerate basement cracking and sump pump upgrades. [[TRANS-2-5]]

## Market 101

| Segment | 2024 TAM | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Structural foundation repair | $5.2B | Piering, wall anchors, slab leveling |
| Basement waterproofing | $3.7B | Interior drainage, sump pumps, vapor barriers |
| Crawlspace encapsulation | $1.6B | High growth (+9% CAGR) in Southeast |

> **Unit economics**: Average repair ticket $12.4k with 2.1 visits, ~45% materials, ~35% labor, rest allocated to sales & G&A. [[DOC-1-9]]

## Competitive Landscape

1. **GroundWorks** – $1.4B revenue roll-up (19 brands). Strength: technician academy + centralized marketing engine. Risk: integration debt from 20+ acquisitions since 2018.
2. **Apex Basement Solutions** – Regional specialist with 65% of bookings from waterproofing; EBITDA margins 24%. [[TRANS-2-8]]
3. **Local independents** – 7,000+ owner-operator outfits averaging <$5M revenue, heavy reliance on Angi/HomeAdvisor leads.

**Quote** (Expert #12): “Basement floods used to be a $4k fix. Now insurers demand vapor barriers + tri-pump systems, pushing average tickets above $10k.” [[TRANS-2-3]]

## Investment Theses

1. **Data-driven lead funnel** – Consolidate fragmented Google/Meta spend into centralized SDR pod; increase close rate from 23% → 31%.
2. **Technician academy** – 8-week standardized curriculum reduces rework by 18% and unlocks expansion into Florida/Southeast markets with higher labor churn.
3. **Warranty-backed maintenance plans** – Subscription sump monitoring (IoT float sensors) adds $480 ARR/home and improves attachment to waterproofing installs.

## Risks & Mitigants

- **Weather volatility** – Mitigated via geographic diversification and financing programs that keep crews utilized in shoulder seasons.
- **Labor scarcity** – Apprentice pipeline + wage progression maps cut attrition to <15% vs. industry 35%.
- **Permitting bottlenecks** – Centralized ops team pre-files with municipalities; digital docs reduce cycle time from 6.2 to 4.1 weeks.
